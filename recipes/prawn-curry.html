Prawn Curry

Description

Make curry in a hurry with this speedy recipe - a fragrant spice pot ready in half an hour
  • Prep time: 10 mins
  • Cook time: 20 mins
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 1 green chilli, deseeded and sliced
  • 3 tbsp curry paste
  • 1 tbsp tomato purée
  • 200ml vegetable stock
  • 200ml coconut cream
  • 350g raw prawn
  • coriander sprigs and rice, to serve

Steps:

  1. Heat the oil in a large frying pan. Fry the onion, garlic and half the chilli for 5 mins or until softened.
  2. Add the curry paste and cook for 1 min more.
  3. Add the tomato purée, stock and coconut cream.
  4. Simmer on medium heat for 10 mins, then add the prawns. Cook for 3 mins or until they turn opaque.
  5. Scatter on the remaining green chillies and coriander sprigs, then serve with rice or cauliflower rice for a keto-friendly version.
